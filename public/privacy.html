Privacy Policy

Thai Good News (TGN) app & website
Last updated: March 5, 2026

Quick version

We collect only what we need to run the app/website, keep it secure, and improve it. We do not sell your personal information.

What we may collect

When you use TGN, we may collect:

  • Device and technical info (like IP address, browser type, and basic diagnostics)
  • Usage info (like which pages/features are used and general app performance)
  • Info you choose to send us (like your name/email if you contact us)

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to understand what’s working, fix bugs, and make the experience better. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work as smoothly.

How we use information

We use information to:

  • Keep the app and website working reliably
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Respond when you contact us
  • Protect users and prevent abuse or security issues

Who we share information with

We don’t sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when it’s necessary to operate the service, such as:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, email tools) who help us run TGN
  • Legal requirements (if we are required to comply with law or protect safety)
If TGN links to other websites or services, they have their own privacy policies.

Keeping info safe

We take reasonable steps to protect your information and limit access to what’s needed. No system is perfect, but we work to keep things secure and up to date.

Your choices

You can ask us to:

  • Tell you what information we have about you
  • Correct it if something is wrong
  • Delete it when possible (some info may need to be kept for legal/security reasons)
