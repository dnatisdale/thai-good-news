# Thai Good News App - Sidebar Guide (User Manual Outline)

![Sidebar Menu](screenshots/01_Sidebar.png)

This guide organizes the Sidebar pages into three main categories: **Core Exploration**, **Personalized Content**, and **Tools & Settings**. This structure helps explain the app's powerful features simply.

---

## 1. Core Exploration (Finding Content)
*These tools help you find the Good News you want to share or listen to.*

### Search
![Search Page](screenshots/02_Search.png)
A quick-access tool. Instead of scrolling through the 100+ languages on the Home screen, you can just type a language name, a message title, or even a keyword here to find exactly what you need instantly.

### Sign Language Video
![Sign Language Page](screenshots/03_Sign_Language.png)
*(Has a "NEW" badge)*: A dedicated page that provides quick access to visual Good News content specifically tailored for the Deaf community in Thailand.

---

## 2. Personalized Content (Your Dashboard)
*These pages are all about saving things for later. You can think of these as your "Personal Dashboard."*

### My Favorites
![My Favorites](screenshots/04_My_Favorites.png)
A quick-bookmark page. If you find a language you use often (like Isan) or a specific message you love, you can tap the "Heart" icon. It gets saved here so you don't have to search for it again.

### My Notes
![My Notes](screenshots/05_My_Notes.png)
A private journaling space. You can jot down thoughts, sermon notes, or reminders about who you want to share a specific message with.

### Selected Messages
![Selected Messages](screenshots/06_Selected_Messages.png)
Think of this page like a **"Shopping Cart" for Sharing**. As you browse the app, you can check the little boxes next to different messages across multiple languages. This page gathers all those checked items into one clean list. From here, you can easily copy the whole list, share it all at once, or print out QR code cards for those specific messages.

---

## 3. Offline Listening & Storage
*These two pages work together closely. The Storage Management page is essentially a settings subpage for My Library.*

### My Library (Downloaded Messages)
![My Library](screenshots/07_My_Library.png)
This is where offline audio lives. When you tap "Download" on an audio track, it saves the file directly to your phone. Go to *My Library* when you are offline (like on a remote mountain or in airplane mode) to play the messages you've saved.

### Storage Management
![Storage Management](screenshots/08_Storage_Management.png)
Audio files take up space on a phone. This page lets you see how much space your downloads are using and gives you a quick way to delete old tracks to free up storage.

---

## 4. Advanced Tools & App Info
*These are utility pages for interacting with the app itself.*

### Import
![Import Page](screenshots/09_Import.png)
A "Power User" tool. The app contains a curated list of messages, but GRN (Global Recordings Network) has thousands more on their website. If you find a unique Program ID on the GRN website that isn't in the app by default, you can type it in here to securely import it into the app for listening and sharing.

### Feedback
![Feedback Page](screenshots/10_Feedback.png)
A simple contact form page. It pre-fills an email so you can easily send bugs, questions, or feature requests to the development team.

### Share
![Share Page](screenshots/11_Share.png)
While the individual message pages let you share *one* specific audio track, **this page is for sharing the actual App itself**. It provides quick buttons to send the app link via Email, LINE, LinkTree, or the device's native sharing prompt so others can easily install it.

---
*Tip for the Manual: When explaining the flow, it can be helpful to clarify the difference between **Sharing a Message** (done on the individual message page or the Selected Messages shopping cart) versus **Sharing the App** (done via the Share page).*
